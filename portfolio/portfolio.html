Responsive Design:-

I ensure that your website looks great on all devices. Responsive design is an approach in web development that involves creating websites that adapt to the user's behavior and environment based on the device they are using. It utilizes a combination of flexible grids, layouts, images, and CSS media queries to ensure that the website adjusts seamlessly to different screen sizes and resolutions. This design philosophy allows websites to automatically respond to changes in the user's platform and orientation, providing an optimal viewing experience across various devices like laptops, tablets, and smartphones
